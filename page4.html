Consulting

HANDS-ON HEALTH AND SAFETY CONSULTANTS THAT ACTUALLY DO THE WORK FOR YOU!
Compliance Health and Safety are health and safety consultants that specialise in supporting small to medium size businesses who cannot afford to employ a full-time health and safety manager.

We try to understand your business and start by helping you manage the introduction of safety. Initially, we start at a basic common sense level of compliance with the Legislation. Then when you understand the process we continuously improve your health and safety culture to an agreed level.

We are on your side! Fighting your corner!
