TIMSHERQ CONSULTING

Tim SHERQ Consultingl is a dynamic company that provides Safety, Health, Environment, Risk and Quality consultancy services across Africa.
We encourage Legal Compliance on all SHERQ laws and International standards for all sectors of the economy

MISSION STATEMENT

To be the leading provider of safety, health and environmental services to our clients through mentorship, empowerment and training.

VISION
Our vision is to increase Safety, Health, Environment, Risk and Quality awareness among workers and employers and further reduce the high levels of cooperate accidents in Africa.
